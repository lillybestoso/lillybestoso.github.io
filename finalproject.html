CSC 106 Final Project

On the left is my final project. Since it is the Holiday season, I decided to create a winter themed game
called "Snowball Fight." The goal is to catch as many snowballs as you can within the 10 second time limit. You use
your left and right arrow keys to move the bitmoji that is at the bottom. If the snowball falls within the bitmoji coordinates, your score
will increase by one. However, if you let any snowballs hit the ground, your score will decrease by one. After your 10 seconds has passed
your score will be displayed on the screen. Feel free to replay the game as many times as you would like and keep trying to beat your score.

Good luck!
